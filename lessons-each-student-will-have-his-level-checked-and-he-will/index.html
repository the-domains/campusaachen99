Lessons Each student will have his level checked and he will be assigned to one of the lessons with the levels described below. We will be using the IKO Methodology and the course will be following each specific program. At the end, students will be given the IKO certification according to the level achieved.

Level 1 Discovery Level 1 Discovery program - The student will start here if he doesn't have any prior skills. The lessons on this level are of 3h max and can go up to 3-4 students with one instructor. (expected to be achieved in one lesson)

Level 2 Intermediate

Level 2 Intermediate Program - At this level the student starts to go in the water to do body drag and after water starts (expectedto be achieved in 2 to 3 lessons) on this level maximum 2 students per instructor and 3h lesson, if student is alone lesson time is reduced to 1,5h lesson

Level 3 Independent and Level 4 Advanced

Level 3 Independent and Level 4 Advanced - At this level the student is mostly practicing and pro- gressing. Lessons are 3h up to 4 students, if there are less than 2 students the lesson is only 2h. There is a ratio of 1 kite for each 2 students minimum.

Evolution Lessons

Evolution classes are done in 1h, with one of our instructors with the IKO coach level. He will access your level and help you improve using the most variated techniques from radio helmets, to video recording.
